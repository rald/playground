Rules and Regulations

  1. Three absents in one month is automatically dropped in all the subjects taken.

  2. Grading is very easy to understand it is only passed or failed.

  3. You'll get a certificate when you passed all the topics and the teacher is satisfied with your performance.

  4. You can ask the teacher to repeat the topic again and again until you learned it well.

  5. You can suggest a subject and it will be added when there is a teacher available.

  6. You can take any time from MON to SUN between 7am to 12am if it is available but maximum of 3hrs per subject only.

  7. You can only take 3 subjects simultaneously.

  8. If you are dropped due to absents you can enroll again next month.

  9. Fifteen minutes late is considered an absent.

  10. You cannot take the same subject on the same day.

  11. Settle your errands before/after the class so there is no interruptions.

  12. If there is free time you can ask the teacher for consultation.

  13. All of this are for free we do not ask for any fee.
